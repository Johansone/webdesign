Cake measurements

A classic, traditional Latvian style cake consists of vanilla cake layers, vanilla milk and butter cream, and lingonberries jam. This cake has three layers. You can choose from three options for the middle layer - vanilla cake layer, chocolate cake layer, or sugar dough layer. For decorations, you can choose to use fruits, seasonal berries, nuts, chocolate, marmalade, decorations from pate a choux dough and mastic of powder sugar.


  • The price starts at 12 dollars for 2 lb. cake.
  • If you would like to order a cake weighting more than 11 lb., then it is available in a square shape only.
  • Custom shaped cakes are priced depending on their size, shape, decorations, and ingredients.
  • Please call or email for more information on custom orders.
