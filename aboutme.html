About me

My name is Andra Johansone and I am a professional pastry chef. I have been making cakes, pastries, and other bakery products since 1987. I studied the pastry chef and baker profession and different food preparation technologies at Janmuiza State Technical School. I have a lot of experience with different food and beverage service companies. My specialty is cakes, which are usually recognizable in a snap.

I have made all kinds of custom-made cakes in different shapes and sizes. However, my unique "signature" in both taste and look is always noticeable. My cakes are made from all natural products only. Do not use inedible things for decoration, and the amount of sugar is never excessive. I make all decorating elements myself from scratch.
